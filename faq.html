General Questions

What is DevTemple?

DevTemple is a digital marketplace and ecosystem designed for developers, creators, and businesses to buy and sell high-performance digital assets. It functions as a "dual-sided platform" that serves as both a launchpad for creators to monetize their skills and a resource for professionals looking to scale their projects using production-ready tools.

Learn more about our platform
How do I get started with DevTemple?

Getting started is simple:

  • Create an account with your name, email, etc.
  • Browse our marketplace for templates and digital resources.
  • Purchase items using our secure payment system
  • If you're a creator, you can also upload your own products to sell
What are the Community Guidelines?

Our Community Guidelines ensure a safe and respectful environment:

  • Treat others with respect - no harassment or hate speech
  • Upload only original or properly licensed content
  • Provide accurate product descriptions and previews
  • No fake reviews or rating manipulation
  • Report suspicious activity or security issues
  • No promotion of violence, illegal activities, or IP theft
  • Maintain one account per person
  • Get approval before posting advertisements
TERMS > COMMUNITY GUIDELINES
How do I report a product?

If you find a product that violates our guidelines, please report it to office@devtem.org. Please provide:

  • The direct link to the product.
  • A clear description of the issue.

Our Action: We review all reports to ensure platform safety. Action may include listing suspension or removal if a violation is confirmed. Even hidden products must strictly follow our community guidelines.

How does DevTemple investigate reports and what action is taken?

Once a report is submitted to office@devtem.org containing a direct product link and details, we review the listing within 24 to 48 hours.

Depending on the severity of the violation, we will execute the following steps:

  • Temporary Suspension: The asset is pulled from public view/access while we reach out to the creator for clarification.
  • Permanent Deletion: If verified as stolen, destructive, or highly fraudulent, the listing is wiped completely.
  • Account Ban: Creators engaging in malicious uploads or systematic spamming will have their accounts and connected payout systems permanently terminated.

What are my account responsibilities?

As a DevTemple user, you're responsible for:

  • Keeping your login credentials secure
  • Reporting any unauthorized access immediately
  • Ensuring your account information is accurate and up-to-date
  • Complying with our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy
  • Following all applicable laws (NDPR, GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
Can I have multiple accounts?

No, we enforce a "One Account, One You" policy. Creating multiple accounts is against our Community Guidelines and may result in account suspension.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information through your account settings. It's important to keep your details current, especially your email address, to receive important notifications about your purchases and account.

DASHBOARD > SETTINGS
Can I unsubscribe from a creator later?

Yes, you have full control over your feed. If you no longer wish to follow a specific account, simply navigate back to their profile card and tap the "Subscribed" button.

It will revert instantly back to the default "Subscribe" state, removing you safely from their broadcast list.

Why was my account deactivated?

If your account has been deactivated, you'll temporarily lose access to your dashboard, payouts, listings, reviews, and referral benefits.

Frequent causes of deactivation:

  • Invalid, misleading, or trademarked/copyrighted username or display name
  • Excessive violations reported by users or flagged by our moderation system
  • Content that violates our guidelines (offensive, misleading, or prohibited material)
  • Manipulation of pricing, rates, or platform benefits
  • Unrelated or spam link detected in the portfolio URL field
  • AI-generated content submitted without the required AI label disclosure
  • Multiple violations of our asset submission guidelines
  • Unresolved licensing or copyright disputes regarding hosted tools
  • Irregular or suspicious activity detected during payout processing
My account was deactivated — what should I do next?

What happens if unresolved:

  • Pending payouts will be frozen until the account status is resolved
  • Listed assets will be unpublished and removed from public discovery
  • Repeated or unaddressed violations may result in a permanent ban
  • Accounts inactive in appeal for 30+ days may be permanently closed

What you should do:

  • Review our terms of service to pinpoint potential violations
  • Gather your developer profile details, including your dynamic Account ID shown on the deactivation notice
  • Submit a formal review request to the DevTemple administration team at office@devtem.org

For Buyers

How do I purchase items on DevTemple?

To purchase items:

  • Browse our marketplace and find products you like
  • Review product descriptions, images, and previews carefully
  • Add items to your cart
  • Proceed to checkout and complete payment via Paystack
  • Do not close the checkout window until payment is fully processed
  • After successful payment, you'll be redirected back to DevTemple
What should I check before making a purchase?

Before purchasing any item, make sure to:

  • Read the product description thoroughly
  • Examine the cover image and any preview materials
  • Check the license terms for usage rights
  • Verify compatibility with your projects
  • Review seller ratings and feedback
What if I encounter issues with a purchased item?

If you experience problems with a purchased item:

  • First, contact the seller directly through our messaging system
  • If the issue isn't resolved, report harmful or malicious content using the report button on the product page
  • For technical issues preventing access, contact our support team
  • Refunds may be considered for inaccessible content due to technical issues
How do I engage with or support group products?

To support specific causes or group products, follow these steps:

  • Select the product and click "Add to Cart".
  • To contribute more than the base amount, simply adjust the quantity in your cart.
  • Proceed to the top right cart icon to complete your checkout.
What does "Subscribing" to a creator mean?

Subscribing is a free way to follow your favorite digital resource creators and brands on DevTemple. When you click the blue "Subscribe" button on a creator's profile or product listing:

  • You will be instantly added to their official subscriber base.
  • The button will toggle to a clean "Subscribed" state to show you are following them.
  • You get notified whenever they drop new templates, private asset packages, or software updates.
How do I subscribe to an account?

There are two quick ways to subscribe to a brand or creator:

  • From a Product Page: Look directly below the item price. Next to the creator's username, click the small blue "Subscribe" badge.
  • From their Profile: Visit their official shop page (e.g., @devtem.org) and click the large prominent "Subscribe" action button located right under their profile summary metrics.
What is "Live Preview" and how do I use it?

The Live Preview button lets you experience a live, fully functional demonstration of code templates and landing pages directly on DevTemple.

When you click Live Preview on a product card or item modal, our system securely extracts the project files in real-time so you can inspect the layout, test responsiveness, and read the seller's documentation tab.

This ensures you know exactly what you are purchasing before spending your money. If a live preview fails to load or looks suspicious, feel free to use our report option or contact support.

Earnings & Withdrawals

Important information about earnings and withdrawals:

  • A 30% service fee applies to all sales on DevTemple.
  • The minimum withdrawal amount is ₦18,000.
  • The maximum withdrawal amount is ₦250,000 per request.
  • Ensure your bank details are accurate before submitting a withdrawal request.
  • All withdrawal requests are processed manually by our team.

Withdrawal processing time may vary depending on verification, review status, and banking schedules. If your withdrawal delays beyond 2 weeks, kindly contact support.

What is the 30% service charge for sellers?

DevTemple applies a 30% service charge on each sale. This commission covers:

  • Platform maintenance and development
  • Payment processing fees
  • Customer support services
  • Marketing and marketplace promotion
  • Security and data protection measures

For example, if you sell a product for $10, you'll receive $7 after our commission.

How and when do I get paid?

Payout details for sellers:

  • Payouts are processed after you submit a withdrawal request.
  • Payments follow our regular payout schedule (usually once a month).
  • Earnings are calculated after deducting a 30% service fee.
  • Payouts may be delayed or withheld if an account or sale is under review for policy violations.

If you don’t receive your payout or notice any issues, please contact our support team within 30 days.

What are the content requirements for sellers?

As a seller, you must:

  • Upload only original or properly licensed content
  • Provide accurate product descriptions
  • Include a clear cover image representing the product
  • Use screenshots or previews that show the actual product output
  • Avoid misleading visuals or unrelated stock images
  • Price products fairly according to their value
  • Follow our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
What should I do to get verified?

After signing up, go to your dashboard and complete your personal information, then click “Save Changes.” Once we review and confirm your details, your account will be verified.

You can still sell without verification, but getting verified helps you stand out — a verified badge will appear on your posts, showing buyers that you’re a trusted seller. Verified sellers often gain more visibility and buyer confidence.

Why should I get verified?

Getting verified helps you build trust and credibility on the platform. A verified badge shows buyers that your account details have been reviewed and confirmed, making you look more professional and reliable.

Verified sellers often get more visibility, attract more buyers, and enjoy faster sales because customers feel more confident dealing with them.

What is verification?

Verification is the process of confirming your identity and account details to ensure authenticity and safety for all users. It helps maintain a secure and trustworthy marketplace by verifying that sellers are real and their information is accurate.

Once verified, a badge appears on your profile and posts to show buyers you’re an approved and trusted seller.

What does "Hide from home page" mean?

This option allows for private product selling. When enabled, your product is excluded from our global listings and home page. It is only accessible via your dashboard or to anyone who has the direct link.

This is ideal for exclusive items, tickets, or group-specific products where you want to handle the distribution yourself while still utilizing our secure checkout and services.

Note: Hidden products must still comply with our community guidelines. This setting is permanent once the product is published and cannot be changed later.

When should I use the "AI-generated content" tag?

You should enable this if your product (code, images, or text) was significantly created or enhanced using AI tools like ChatGPT or Midjourney.

Transparency builds trust with your buyers. Please ensure this is set correctly before publishing, as product settings cannot be edited after posting.

Can I edit my product after it is published?

Currently, product details and settings cannot be edited once they are live.

This policy ensures that the product buyers see is exactly what they receive and prevents post-purchase confusion. Please double-check your title, description, price, and "Product Settings" before hitting Publish Project.

If you made a serious error, you will need to delete the post and re-upload it.

How do I sell private packages or send products to specific clients?

You can use the "Hide from home page" feature during the upload process to facilitate private sales. This is ideal for:

  • Client Delivery: Sending custom packages directly to clients or teams through DevTemple.
  • Exclusive Sales: Selling items like tickets or group-specific products via a direct link.
  • Secure Transactions: Ensuring your private buyers still benefit from verified and encrypted payments.
What types of digital resources are strictly prohibited?

To maintain a high-quality marketplace for creators and developers, DevTemple does not accept the following:

  • Malicious & Broken Code: Files containing viruses, malware, obfuscated backdoors, or broken, non-functional scripts.
  • Stolen or Plagiarized Assets: Resources uploaded without explicit resale rights, licenses, or authorization from the original creator.
  • Misleading Digital Products: Items with deceptive descriptions, fake previews, or features that do not work as advertised.
  • Prohibited Content types: Digital assets promoting scams, fraud, explicit adult themes, or unsafe behavior.
What are the most common guidelines violations?

Creators should watch out for these frequent mistakes to keep their listings active:

  • Spamming & Duplicate Listings: Uploading the same digital package multiple times with minor title changes to flood the marketplace.
  • Fee Evasion / External Redirection: Including links inside descriptions or download files that encourage users to complete checkout off the DevTemple platform.
  • Misusing "Hide from Home Page": Utilizing private link packages to distribute prohibited materials, bypass licensing checks, or dodge our community standards.
Do "Hidden" or "Private" packages have to follow the rules?

Yes, absolutely. Even if a listing is set to "Hide from home page" for private team deployment or client handoff, it resides on DevTemple's infrastructure.

All private files are processed and checked under the same safety terms. Any private product found violating guidelines will be systematically flagged and removed.

As a creator, how do I see who has subscribed to me?

You can track your community growth right from your profile and dashboard. Your total subscriber count is prominently displayed on your store cards.

By clicking on your "Subscribers" count link, a secure overlay modal will open showing your active fanbase. You can view their profile pictures, display names, and handles (e.g., @RealFigsh, @Huxpower) so you know exactly who is engaging with your resources.

How does the "Notify your subscribers" feature work?

When publishing or drafting a digital resource, you can slide the "Notify your subscribers" toggle switch in your advanced product settings.

Once you click "Publish Project", DevTemple handles the delivery automatically, instantly dispatching an email notification directly to your community codebase with access details to your new post.

Why is the notification toggle disabled or locked for me?

To optimize server payload resources and maintain platform health, the email broadcast toggle is strictly available to creators who have at least 1 active subscriber on their profile.

If your account currently sits at 0 subscribers, this option will remain locked. As soon as a peer or client hits the "Subscribe" button on your profile card, the system dynamically unlocks the mailing feature for your subsequent uploads.

Are there any restrictions on sending subscriber notifications?

While notifying followers is an incredible way to scale conversion funnels on private packages or new scripts, please keep these structural guidelines in mind:

  • Permanent States: Once a project is officially published with the notification toggle active, the emails are queued instantly. Because post details cannot be changed post-launch, verify your download files and pricing structures thoroughly beforehand.
  • Anti-Spam Thresholds: Utilizing rapid duplicate uploads (spamming the same digital file to re-trigger notifications) triggers system flags, leading to automated listing suspension or account investigation.
How does the Live Preview feature work for my products?

The Live Preview allows potential buyers to test and interact with your digital template directly from their browser before completing a purchase.

To make sure your project loads correctly in the preview window:

  • Your uploaded ZIP file must include a root index.html file.
  • Ensure all your internal assets, style directories, and scripts use proper relative file paths (e.g., ./css/style.css).
  • Include a clean, descriptive readme.md file if your code requires special configuration or documentation.

Providing a working live preview drastically increases buyer confidence, builds trust, and helps your items stand out in the marketplace.

Privacy & Security

What information does DevTemple collect?

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and country
  • Payment Information: Payment history via Paystack to track transactions
  • Usage Data: Cookies, session data, and analytics to improve user experience

Passwords are never stored in plaintext - only encrypted versions are maintained for security.

How is my data protected?

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive information (passwords, payment details)
  • Secure handling of payment data by Paystack
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data for authorized personnel only

However, no system is completely secure, so users should also protect their account credentials.

What are my privacy rights?

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Receive your data in a portable format (GDPR)
  • Opt out of data sharing (we don't sell your data)
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

What should I do if my payment is failed or stuck?

If you encounter a transaction issue, please share the following with our support team:

  • The payment method used.
  • The transaction ID or reference code from Paystack.
  • The asset name you were attempting to purchase.
How are cookies used on DevTemple?

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Managing user sessions and core functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Analyzing platform performance and usage
  • Functional Cookies: Providing personalized content and recommendations

Under GDPR, we require explicit consent for non-essential cookies. You can manage preferences via the cookie consent popup or browser settings.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

DevTemple uses Paystack for payment processing, which supports various payment methods including:

  • Credit and debit cards
  • Bank transfers
  • Mobile money (in supported regions)
  • Other local payment options depending on your country
What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy:

  • Payments are generally non-refundable
  • Refunds may be considered if technical issues prevent access to purchased content
  • If Paystack experiences issues (failed debit, double charge), they handle refunds according to their policies
  • Refunds may be approved if content is proven fraudulent, harmful, or misrepresented
  • Disputes must be reported within 30 days of purchase

We encourage buyers to review products carefully before purchasing.

What happens if I close the payment window too early?

If you close the checkout window before payment is fully processed:

  • Your purchase may not be recorded
  • It can be difficult to trace or refund incomplete transactions
  • You might be charged without receiving the product

Always wait for the payment confirmation and redirection back to DevTemple before closing any windows.

Who should I contact for payment issues?

For payment-related issues:

  • First, check your transaction email
  • If the issue is with Paystack (failed payments, double charges), contact Paystack support
  • For other payment issues or if you didn't receive purchased content, contact DevTemple support
  • Include transaction details and screenshots when reporting issues

Referral Program

How does the DevTemple Affiliate / Referral Program work?

The DevTemple referral program allows you to earn passive income by sharing digital assets with your network. It works in three simple steps:

  1. Go to your dashboard reward section and click "Copy" to grab your unique referral link.
  2. Share the link with creators, developers, or groups who need digital resources.
  3. When someone visits DevTemple through your link and completes a purchase, you automatically earn a 7% commission on every single item they buy.
Where can I see my earnings?

Your earnings accumulate in real-time under your Available reward Balance, which is visible at the top of your rewards dashboard in both light and dark modes.

This balance tracking system keeps your referral earnings entirely independent of your main vendor or store balances, making it easier to track your marketing performance.

DASHBOARD > REWARDS
How do I request a withdrawal for my rewards?

Once you've accumulated earnings, you can transfer your reward balance directly to your bank account:

  • Click the blue "Withdraw" button next to your available balance.
  • A secure pop-up form will appear requesting your payout details.
  • Fill in the required information carefully: Country, Bank Name, Account Name, and Account Number.
  • Click "Submit Withdrawal" to send the request to our admin team for verification and payout.
Are there any limits or conditions on payouts?

Yes, to maintain security and ensure a smooth transaction processing line, please note the following:

  • Verification Check: Your account name on DevTemple should match your legal bank account details to prevent payment failures or fraud flags.
  • Optional Details: You can use the Address and Additional Notes fields on the form to provide extra delivery/routing instructions if you are using specialized banking setups or international routes.
  • Policy Compliance: Promoting your affiliate link via spamming, misleading descriptions, or automated bots is strictly prohibited under our platform guidelines and will result in a permanent forfeiture of rewards.
